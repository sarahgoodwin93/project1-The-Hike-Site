About

The Hike Site is by hikers for hikers. A place you can come to in order to get information for trip to bonny Scotland. Whether it is a day hike, multi-day wild camping trip or something inbetween, The Hike Site has helpful tips and tricks to make your adventure the best it can be.


Visit our Suggested Walks page for links to some of our favourite walks in some of the best areas in Scotland. We have river walks, Munro summits, loop tracks, forest settings are more!


Check out our What to Pack page to make sure you've got your gear sorted for your next trip, we even have a hand checklist you can fill out for each trip, nothing worse than forgetting something when you are on the top of a hill!
